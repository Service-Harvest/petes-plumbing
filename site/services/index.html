Our Services

Whatever's going on with your plumbing, there's a good chance we've handled it before. Here's a breakdown of what we do for homeowners and small commercial properties across Armonk and Westchester County.

Pete's Plumbing covers the plumbing needs of homes and small businesses throughout Armonk and the surrounding towns. That includes everyday repairs, drain and sewer work, gas line service, septic-connected plumbing, and the plumbing side of bathroom and kitchen remodels. Every job starts with a clear look at what's actually causing the problem, followed by an upfront price before we touch anything. Use the categories below to find the service that matches what's happening at your property.

Drainage Service

Slow drains, sewer backups, and stubborn clogs are some of the most common calls we get from Armonk-area homeowners. Our drainage service overview covers everything from routine drain cleaning for everyday buildup to sewer line repair and diagnosis when a backup points to something more serious underground. For grease, scale, or root growth that a snake can't clear on its own, we also offer hydro jetting for tough clogs. When a clog keeps coming back, tracking down a recurring clog's real cause and a rooter service for stubborn blockages go deeper than a plunger can. We can also confirm what's happening underground with a camera inspection of your sewer and drain lines, and for restaurants and small commercial kitchens we offer grease trap cleaning and maintenance.

Gasfitter

Gas work isn't something to guess at, which is why our gasfitter services overview walks through exactly how our licensed technicians handle it. If you smell gas, notice a sudden pressure drop, or just need a gas line leak repair, we respond quickly and explain what we find along the way. We also handle gas water heater installation for homes installing a new unit or switching over from electric.

Septic Services

Homes on septic systems need a plumber who understands how the two systems work together. Our septic-connected plumbing support covers the plumbing side of septic issues, from slow drains to backups near the tank, and our septic tank service details page explains what we check and how we walk you through the problem before recommending any work.

Bathroom & Kitchen Remodeling

Planning a renovation? Our remodeling plumbing services cover the plumbing side of your project, from moving supply lines to rerouting drains behind the walls. That includes bathroom remodel plumbing work like new shower valves and toilet relocations, plus kitchen remodel plumbing work like sink moves and new dishwasher hookups.

Plumbing

General plumbing is still the biggest part of what we do day to day. Our full range of plumbing services includes everyday repairs and installations in every room of the house, grouped below so you can find what you need.

Emergency, Leak & Pipe Repair

When something can't wait, we offer 24/7 emergency plumbing repair for burst pipes and active leaks, including stopping a burst pipe fast and thawing a pipe frozen by a cold snap before it cracks. If you suspect trouble you can't see, we offer hidden leak detection, repairing a leak hidden under your foundation, and installing sensors that flag a leak early. For damaged or aging pipework, we handle patching a section of damaged pipe, swapping out pipe that keeps failing, replacing the line that feeds your home, and fixing a break in your water main. If pressure has dropped throughout the house, our low water pressure diagnosis and repair finds the real cause.

Water Heating, Softening & Filtration

Our new water heater installation service is one of our most requested jobs, alongside going tankless for on-demand hot water and repairing a hot water system that's underperforming. We also handle choosing and installing a new boiler. For water quality, we offer softening hard water before it damages pipes and filtering water for cleaner taps.

Fixture, Appliance & Bathroom/Kitchen Installs

We install and repair the fixtures homeowners touch every day: setting up a new toilet, fixing a running or clogged toilet, putting in a new kitchen or bath faucet, and repairing a leaking outdoor spigot. We also handle installing a new sink, setting a new bathtub in place, building out a new shower, and replacing a shower valve that won't hold temperature. Rounding this out: swapping in updated plumbing fixtures, fixing a jammed garbage disposal, hooking up a new dishwasher or washing machine, and installing a urinal for a commercial restroom.

Specialty, Maintenance & Commercial

We also cover work that doesn't fit neatly into a single category: sizing and installing a sump pump to protect a basement, setting up a rainwater collection tank, and running supply and drain lines for a laundry room. For commercial and compliance needs, we provide annual backflow testing and documentation, a full plumbing inspection before you buy or sell, a routine plumbing maintenance visit, plumbing support for small commercial properties, and repiping a home with recurring pipe failures.

Not sure which service fits your problem?

Call us and we'll help you figure out exactly what you need.
